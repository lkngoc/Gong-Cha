From Taiwan to the world

GONG CHA

- GREAT TEA, FRESHLY PRESENTED

The essence of Gong Cha, or Tribute Tea, originated from the provision of the most premium and refined teas to the Emperor of China. It represents the highest quality and expectations of beverages. Established in Taiwan in 2006, Gong Cha has become deeply appreciated by its customers, gaining global renown by word of mouth and a uniquely customized services.


Today, Gong Cha outlets around the world number near 1500, having become a widespread sensational concept among various consumers. Gong Cha's story is one that roots itself as one of the most reliable providers of quality tea in the world.
